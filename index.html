Six Skiers You Must Follow during Winter 2020

By Olivier Lemire
Last updated : 2019-11-28
Responsive versions for Ipad and Iphone, Best on Laptop

1. Phil Casabon

Originaly from Quebec, this skier embraces the baggy style with ease. What I enjoy about his skiing is how aggressive he is on rails. He produces his own ski movies with high quality production which makes it even a better skier as he takes as much time to ski and to produce his movies. It is good for the ski community in Quebec to have one of the best skier of this generation representing them. He is sponsored by multiple companies but most importantly one named Armada, a company that is known world wide to make incredebly good skis. Following Casabon this winter will give you the opportunity to watch, in my opinion, one of the best ski movies a skier has to offer. Plus, supporting a Quebec skier is good for our ski community.

Access his insta and his movie 'Nuance' here:

2. Siver Voll

Voll brings to the mountain the best that skiing has to offer. His style mixes elements from aggressive to swervy with ease. I first heard about him through his superunknown videos, a ski contest for the best edits. When he got on the ON3P team I started following him with more intensity and he soon became one of my favorite skier. His appearances in the third ON3P movie blew my mind away and I still have the same reactions after replaying the video so many times. His 19-20 season recap was amazing too, which is why following him this season will ensure you don't miss his new tricks until he drops next season's recap.

Access his insta and his edit for superunknown here:

3. Magnus Graner

Magnus is the skier who got me interested in freeskiing. 3 years ago, I didn't know how big of an influence skiing would be on my life. All this is possible because of one video on instagram I saw. It was Magnus doing "wierd things (As I thought 3 years ago)" with his skis that made me buy new skis and decided to give all my will to try harder and harder things. Looking back at it, I would not be in love with skiing as much as I am today without Magnus. I recommend everyone to follow him to support him as I think I owe him that. His loose style makes me enjoy skiing doing things other people on the hill find funny. Overall, he is a really good skier that has a special place in my heart because his videos got me into skiing.

Access his insta and one of his edit :

4. Ian aka SkrtSkrtSwerve

SkrtSkrtSwerve is a skier that I look up a lot to. Mostly because his style is my unique by being loose and wearing really cool outfits. Unfortunately, Ian does not post as much as the other skier on the list on instagram. This is sad because I could watch his videos all day. We can see him in edits of other people and on the ON3P movies from time to time. If he had more content, he would be my favorite skier. I recommend following him because who knows, maybe he will post a lot of picture this season ?!

Access his insta and his video part in ON3P 3 :

5. Jake Mageau

Jake Mageau is a skier that rides for ON3P like some others on the list. He won the viewers vote in the last real ski contest. This video was so insane I watched it like 10 times. He never stops bringing new content that amazes me. I absolutly love his style because it is so unique. He is a big part of the new ON3P movie which dropped not so long ago. This movie proves that he isnt just good at rails. In fact, the movie was only powder shots and they were all amazing. I hope more is to come this season from Jake.

Access his insta and one of his real ski :

6. Henrik Harlaut

Henrik as the og style of skiing. I love this skier mostly for the happiness he let go when he skis. He seems to have a really good sport attitude which I think is an important aspect to an athlete. His movies and edits are always so good because of the editing, the music but mostly the skiing. To my eyes, he is probably the best mainstream skier when it comes to park skiing. It is important to follow him this season because his next movie "Salute" is going to drop soon.
